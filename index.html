Top Destinations You Must Visit

Kedarnath:

It is famous for Lord Shiva's Temple and a pilgrimage site in the Himalayas, home to the Kedarnath Temple its location amidst majestic snow-capped peaks. The surrounding natural beauty, attracting evotess from all around the world.

  • Kedarnath Temple
  • Bhairavnath Temple
  • Vasuki Tal

Tirupati:

Tirupati is famous for the Tirumala Venkateswara Temple, a renowned Hindu pilgrimage site dedicated to Lord Venkateswara, making it one of the most important religious centers in India. The city is also home to other significant temples like the Padmavathi Temple and Govindaraja Swamy Temple, and is known for its spiritual significance, rich history, and the famous Srivari Laddu prasadam.

  • Sri Venkateshwara
  • Chandragiri Fort
  • Talakona Waterfall

Dwarka:

Dwarka is famous as the ancient, legendary capital of Lord Krishna's kingdom, a major Hindu pilgrimage site and one of the Char Dham holy sites, home to the ancient Dwarkadhish Temple dedicated to Krishna, and for its spiritual significance on the western coast of India.

  • Nageshwar Temple
  • Jyotirlinga Temple
  • Geeta Mandir

Amritsar:

Amritsar is famous for the Golden Temple, a holy Sikh site, and the Jallianwala Bagh massacre site, a significant historical landmark. The city also attracts visitors with its famous cuisine, such as Amritsari Kulcha and lassi, and its lively markets where you can find Phulkari dupattas and traditional jewelry. The daily Wagah border ceremony is another major attraction.

  • Golden Temple
  • Jallianwala Bagh Memorial
  • Partition museum
